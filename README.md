# Hospital 360 Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Recommended Structure and Order

---

# 1. Project Title / Headline

A comprehensive healthcare analytics dashboard built in Power BI to monitor hospital operations, patient flow, doctor performance, treatment trends, and operational KPIs through interactive visualizations and business intelligence reporting.

---

# 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Hospital 360 Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end healthcare reporting solution designed to help hospitals and healthcare management teams analyze patient records, admission trends, occupancy rates, treatment statistics, and operational performance.

The dashboard enables better decision-making by providing a centralized view of hospital activities and performance metrics.

---

# 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 Power BI Desktop — Main data visualization platform used for dashboard creation
- 🔄 Power Query — Data transformation, cleaning, and modeling
- 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) — Used for KPIs, calculated measures, and dynamic visuals
- 📁 Excel / CSV Dataset — Data source used for healthcare records and reporting
- 📈 Interactive Visuals — KPI cards, charts, slicers, and drill-through analysis

---

# 4. Data Source

The dashboard uses hospital-related datasets containing information such as:

- Patient admissions & discharge records
- Department details
- Doctor information
- Bed occupancy
- Treatment and diagnosis data
- Billing and revenue details

The dataset is structured to simulate real-world hospital operational reporting and healthcare analytics.

---

# 5. Features / Highlights

## • Business Problem

Hospitals generate large amounts of patient and operational data daily. Managing and analyzing this information manually becomes difficult and time-consuming.

Healthcare administrators require a centralized analytics solution to monitor hospital performance, patient trends, and operational efficiency.

---

## • Goal of the Dashboard

To provide an interactive and dynamic analytics platform for monitoring hospital KPIs and improving operational decision-making.

The dashboard helps management track patient statistics, doctor performance, occupancy rates, revenue insights, and department-level analysis.

---

## • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### 📌 KPI Cards
- Total Patients
- Total Admissions
- Occupancy Rate
- Revenue Generated
- Average Length of Stay

### 📌 Patient Trend Analysis
Line charts showing patient admission and discharge trends over time.

### 📌 Department Performance
Comparison of departments based on patient count, treatment volume, and operational load.

### 📌 Doctor Analysis
Performance metrics and patient handling statistics for doctors.

### 📌 Bed Occupancy Analysis
Visualization of occupied vs available beds and ward utilization.

### 📌 Revenue Insights
Billing and revenue analysis across departments and treatments.

---

## • Business Impact & Insights

- Helps hospital management monitor operational efficiency
- Improves patient flow management
- Enables faster healthcare reporting
- Assists in identifying high-load departments
- Supports data-driven healthcare decisions
- Enhances resource allocation and planning
